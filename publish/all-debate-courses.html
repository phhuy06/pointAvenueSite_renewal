DEBATE COURSES

Our Debate Program aims to nurture future leaders and their ability to think critically, collaborate, express and share their ideas in a powerful manner. Throughout the term, students will be exposed to a variety of debating styles, with a particular focus on World Schools Debating Championships (WSDC) and British Parliamentary (BP) formats.

Our unique debate program harnesses the skills and experience of award-winning debaters, internationally recognized coaches, and a team comprised of experienced coaches from around the globe. Through our collective experiences, skill sets, and methods, we can deliver one of a kind curriculum to students tailored to maximize their personal growth.
