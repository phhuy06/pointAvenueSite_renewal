SCHOLARSHIP

Become Point Avenue’s Scholar!

Our mission is "to empower tomorrow's leaders to transform the world through wisdom, integrity and compassion". With thsis goal, we seek out and provide hard-working, compassionate students with the guidance to gain entrance into their dream universities, and become leaders who positively impact their communities.

Who can Apply?
